Heart Maintance

Revitalize your cardiovascular system with our Young Heart Elixir. This comprehensive multi-herb tonic masterfully blends hawthorn, turmeric, ginger, and garlic to provide all-around heart support. Each ingredient works synergistically to promote vitality, circulation, and long-term cardiac wellness.

1 tbsp dried hawthorn berries, 1 inch fresh turmeric or 1/2 tsp powder, 1 inch fresh ginger or 1/2 tsp powder, 1-2 cloves garlic, Juice of 1/2 lemon, Pinch cayenne pepper (optional), 1-2 tsp honey (optional), 1 cup water

1. If using fresh turmeric and ginger, peel and thinly slice. 2. Bring 1 cup water to boil. 3. Add hawthorn berries, turmeric, ginger, and garlic to boiling water. 4. Mix well and enjoy warm. 10. Store leftovers in fridge 1-2 days.
